Complete Gynecology Practice Management

All the tools you need for comprehensive women's health care

Comprehensive Gynecology EMR

Document Chief Complaints, Menstrual & Sexual History, Clinical Observations, and Diagnosis. Record Vitals, LMP, Obstetric History (G, P, A, L), and Menopause details with structured forms.

Smart Appointment Management

Schedule consultations, antenatal checkups, procedures, or scans. Send appointment reminders via WhatsApp or Email with queue/token system management.

Personalized Prescriptions & Treatment Plans

Use preloaded gynecology-specific medicine templates with dosage, frequency, and duration. Share via WhatsApp, Email, or print with digital signature.

Pregnancy & Antenatal Visit Tracker

Track pregnancy week-by-week with checklists and vitals. Document fetal heart rate, fundal height, scan findings, and weight gain with trimester-specific protocols.

Easy Document Sharing & Patient Communication

Share prescriptions, reports, invoices, case papers instantly on WhatsApp or Email. Send appointment confirmations, payment receipts, and checkup recalls.

Billing & Payment Made Simple

Generate invoices for consultation, scans, procedures, deliveries, or packages. Offer antenatal or infertility treatment packages with installment tracking.

Multi-User Access for Clinic Staff

Add junior doctors, nurses, receptionists, lab assistants, billing staff with role-based access. Work from mobile, tablet, or desktop with real-time data sync.

Analytics & Reports for Informed Decisions

View doctor-wise, procedure-wise, branch-wise reports. Financial summaries for revenue, outstanding dues, and antenatal care follow-through tracking.
