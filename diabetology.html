Clinicia for Diabetologists

Simplify Diabetes Care with Smart EMR & Clinic Management

Managing diabetes involves continuous monitoring, lifestyle guidance, regular follow-ups, and lab investigations. Clinicia is designed to help Diabetologists streamline their clinic operations while providing highly personalized, efficient, and long-term care for chronic patients.

Complete Diabetology Practice Management

Specialized features designed specifically for diabetes care and chronic patient management

Diabetes-Specific Clinical Documentation

Capture Chief Complaints, Vitals, Blood Sugar, BMI, BP, HbA1c and other key metrics with structured templates for new and follow-up visits.

  • Maintain detailed Diagnosis Notes and Ongoing Treatment Plans
  • Add lab test results: Fasting/PP glucose, HbA1c, Lipid Profile, Renal function
  • Upload external reports and annotate test trends
  • Maintain longitudinal patient history for chronic case monitoring

Follow-Up Scheduling & Reminders

Schedule routine diabetic checkups (monthly, quarterly) in advance with automated patient reminders and compliance tracking.

  • Send appointment reminders via WhatsApp or Email
  • Auto-remind patients about missed follow-ups or overdue tests
  • Customize follow-up timelines based on patient condition
  • Track patient compliance with follow-ups and monitor appointment trends

Smart Prescription Management

Preloaded diabetic drug templates and treatment combinations for efficient prescription management with professional documentation.

  • Preloaded templates: Metformin, Insulin, Gliptins
  • Save combinations for common treatment plans
  • Prescriptions printed or shared via WhatsApp with clinic branding
  • Include personalized dosage instructions and lifestyle advice

Patient Communication Made Easy

Comprehensive communication tools for long-term chronic diabetes management and patient engagement.

  • Share prescriptions, lab test requests, and progress summaries instantly
  • Automated WhatsApp reminders for medication refills and test appointments
  • Welcome/thank-you messages and birthday greetings
  • Long-term communication for chronic diabetes management

Billing & Payment Tracking

Comprehensive billing system for consultations, insulin training, dietary counseling, and investigations with instant receipts.

  • Bill for consultations, insulin training, dietary counseling
  • Collect partial/full payments and send instant receipts
  • Role-based access for billing and collection activities
  • Financial reports: revenue, dues, trends in patients

Powerful Reports & Analytics

Monitor trends in HbA1c levels, glucose readings, and patient visit frequency with comprehensive reporting and data export.

  • Monitor trends in HbA1c levels and glucose readings
  • Financial and operational reports by doctor and date range
  • Assess new vs repeat patients, checkup compliance
  • Data export with privacy-controlled access
