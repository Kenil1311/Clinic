Comprehensive Nephrology Practice Management

Everything you need to manage chronic kidney care with precision and continuity

Kidney-Centric EMR Tailored for Nephrologists

Record detailed clinical notes, track chronic kidney disease (CKD) stages, and maintain comprehensive patient documentation.

  • Track CKD stages, GFR, Creatinine, Urea, Electrolytes
  • Document investigations like USG Abdomen, KUB, Renal Doppler, Renal Biopsy
  • Upload lab reports, images, and historical data for longitudinal care

Dialysis Management

Schedule Hemodialysis or Peritoneal Dialysis sessions with automatic reminders and comprehensive session tracking.

  • Record session details: date, duration, dry weight, UF goal, BP, complications
  • Track vascular access (AV fistula, catheter), maintenance, and follow-up
  • Generate and share dialysis summaries and invoices

Follow-Up & Chronic Disease Monitoring

Schedule routine reviews, lab tests, and monitor CKD progression with automated reminders.

  • Monitor CKD progression using custom forms or pre-loaded templates
  • Send automated follow-up and test reminders via WhatsApp, Email, or SMS
  • Monitor adherence to fluid, diet, medication, and lifestyle protocols

Patient Communication & Engagement

Send prescriptions, diet charts, and educational materials to patients for better care compliance.

  • Send diet charts, payment receipts, reports via WhatsApp or Email
  • Remind patients about upcoming dialysis sessions or missed appointments
  • Share educational material on kidney care, diet management, and CKD awareness

Billing, Packages & Payments

Generate invoices for consultations, dialysis sessions, investigations, and procedures with flexible payment options.

  • Accept full or partial payments with auto-confirmation on WhatsApp
  • Apply discounts or insurance-based billing based on membership or plan
  • Download or share compliant invoices and receipts instantly

MIS & Reports for Nephrology Practices

Analyze patient volume by CKD stage, dialysis type, and track comprehensive practice metrics.

  • Track missed appointments, repeat visits, test frequency trends
  • Financial reports for revenue, outstanding payments, and insurance claims
  • Export MIS data in Excel or PDF for internal review or external audits
