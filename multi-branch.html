Complete Multi-Branch Management Capabilities

Everything you need to seamlessly manage multiple clinic locations

Branch-Wise Operations

Assign separate teams, schedules, services, and pricing per branch. Customize workflows for each clinic while maintaining central oversight. Seamless coordination of appointments, EMRs, billing, and stock at each location.

One Unified Platform

Real-Time Data Sync

Patient data, appointments, billing, and communications sync instantly. Doctors and admin teams can access the latest data, whether they're at HQ or a satellite branch. Easily transfer patient records when they visit another branch.

Instant Synchronization

Centralized EMR

Shared patient profiles with branch-specific visit history. Track treatments, diagnostics, prescriptions, and documents from any location. Role-based access control ensures data privacy and confidentiality.

Branch-Level Access

Branch-Wise Scheduling

Maintain independent calendars for each clinic. Online booking links can be customized per location. Manage token systems, queue flow, reschedules, and no-shows branch-wise with WhatsApp reminders.

Location-Specific Booking

Financial Management

Generate bills, estimates, and receipts per branch. Monitor collections, dues, discounts, and tax details individually. Track branch-wise performance and doctor-wise revenue with segregated expense management.

Multi-Branch Analytics

Access branch-wise, doctor-wise, and department-wise reports. Financial and non-financial metrics: revenue, appointments, patient trends, and more. Download reports in Excel or PDF with controlled access.
