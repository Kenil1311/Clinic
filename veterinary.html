Complete Veterinary Practice Management

Everything you need to run a modern veterinary clinic efficiently

Complete Animal Patient Records

Maintain detailed animal EMRs including species, breed, gender, age, weight, and health history. Store owner details with emergency contacts. Track vaccination history, allergies, diagnosis, and treatment plans.

Vaccination & Deworming Schedule Management

Preloaded canine, feline, avian, equine, and livestock vaccination schedules. Automatic reminders for upcoming vaccines via WhatsApp or Email. Record batch numbers, brands, and expiry dates.

Appointment Scheduling with Pet Owner Alerts

Schedule check-ups, vaccinations, surgeries, grooming sessions, or follow-ups. Send reminders and confirmations on WhatsApp or Email. Display token numbers and manage waiting queues.

Communication with Pet Owners

Share documents like prescriptions, invoices, payment receipts, and medical records. Send welcome messages, routine care tips, diet plans, and auto-schedule reminders for annual vaccinations.

Billing & Invoicing

Create detailed itemized invoices for consultations, treatments, procedures, and medicines. Collect payments and issue receipts instantly via WhatsApp. Offer package pricing for wellness plans.

Inventory Management for Veterinary Supplies

Manage stocks of medications, vaccines, surgical supplies, pet food, grooming items. Get alerts for low stock items or expiring medicines. Generate daily usage reports.

Reports & Analytics

Financial reports for revenues, payments, and dues. Clinical reports on cases handled, procedures performed, species/breed treated. Vaccine trends and follow-up success rates.

Role-Based Access for Vets and Staff

Define access levels for veterinarians, assistants, receptionists, storekeepers. Allow multi-user access with real-time data synchronization. Use on desktop, Android, iOS devices.
