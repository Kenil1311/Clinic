What You Can Do with Clinicia's Membership Plans

Comprehensive membership tools to build loyalty and recurring revenue

Create Custom Membership Programs

Design plans tailored to your practice and patient demographics. Define duration, validity, and renewal terms. Offer annual, bi-annual, or custom duration memberships. Assign unique pricing and names to each plan—whether it's a Basic Care Plan or a Premium Smile Package.

Offer Discounts on Total Bills or Specific Treatments

Set flat discounts or percentage-based discounts. Apply benefits to entire invoices / total bill amount, or specific procedures/treatments (e.g. scaling, root canal, diagnostics). Flexible enough to support every speciality—from dental to dermatology.

Link Memberships to Patient Profiles

Easily tag a membership to a patient during registration or billing. Auto-apply benefits during billing—no manual calculations required. See active/inactive memberships at a glance during appointments or payments. Seamless integration that saves staff time and improves accuracy.

Boost Renewals and Re-Engagement

Track upcoming expiry dates of memberships. Send automated renewal reminders via WhatsApp or Email. Offer renewal-only deals to retain valuable patients. Keep patients returning—and revenue recurring.

Analyze Membership Impact

Reports on membership uptake, revenue generated from members, discounts offered, and active vs expired plans. Know what's working and optimize future offerings.
